About Me

I am Lakpriya Weragoda, an Undergraduate from the University of Peradeniya.

I am currently specializing in Mechatronics Engineering in the Department of Mechanical Engineering, Faculty of Engineering, University of Peradeniya. I am interest in areas like Robotics, Automation and Embedded Systems. I am also interested in sports, specially Badminton.

Education

Royal International School, Kurunegala was the place where I got my primary education from grade 1 to grade 11. And it was the place that guided me to obtain good results in GCE Ordinary Level examination in 2014.

Then I went to Maliyadeva College, Kurunegala, where I was guided to do my GCE Adavanced Level examination in 2017 in the Physical Science Stream. I was successful in myfirst attempt and I got selected to the Faculty of Engineering, University of Peradeniya.

In the first year of the University, I was able to get a good result and get in to a field of my choosing. I choose the feild of Mechatronics Engineering offered by the Mechanical Engineering Department of the Faculty of Engineering, University of Peradeniya.

During my undergraduate period I was able to work as an intern at MAS Holding Ratmalana during my internship period. Currently I am a third year undergraduate in the Mechanical Engineering Department, Faculty of Engineering, University of Peradeniya.
